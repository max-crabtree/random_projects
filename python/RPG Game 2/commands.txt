++GENERAL INTERACTION COMMANDS++

move/mv [direction]: moves the player in [direction]
         direction: north OR south OR east OR west

look/lk: look around yourself

inventory/inv: check your inventory

stats/s: view player statistics

---------------------------------------------------------------

**BATTLE COMMANDS**

attack/a: attack the monster currently being fought

consume/c: consume an item from inventory

defend/d: defend against the enemy

run/r: attempt to run from the enemy
